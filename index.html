ABOUT F1

Formula 1 (F1) features the fastest and most advanced single-seater cars in the world. Teams compete in a series of races, known as Grand Prix, held on circuits across the globe. Each team designs its car and employs two drivers who race to earn points in both the Drivers' and Constructors' Championships. The sport combines cutting-edge technology with strategic planning and driver skill, making it a thrilling and highly competitive spectacle.

2023 WINNERS

Drivers' Championship

Max

Verstappen

Max Verstappen, the driver for Red Bull Racing, clinched his third consecutive title in 2023 with unparalleled dominance, winning 19 out of the 22 races in the season. He amassed a remarkable 575 points, surpassing even the combined total of the season's runner-up. The top 3 drivers were:

  1. 1. Max Verstappen (Red Bull) with 575 points
  2. 2. Sergio Perez (Red Bull) with 285 points
  3. 3. Lewis Hamilton (Mercedes) with 234 points

Constructors' Championship

Red Bull

Racing

Red Bull Racing had an extraordinary season in 2023, clinching the Constructors' Championship with an impressive total of 860 points. Their dominance was evident as they won all the races except for one, the Singapore Grand Prix. The RB19 , Red Bull's 2023 car, proved to be a masterpiece of engineering, offering a perfect blend of speed, reliability, and aerodynamic efficiency. The top 3 were:

  1. 1. Red Bull with 860 points
  2. 2. Mercedes with 409 points
  3. 3. Ferrari with 406 points
