My favourite events in F1 history

October 2013
Iconic Vettel in India

Sebastian Vettel secured a commanding victory at the 2013 Indian Grand Prix, held at the Buddh International Circuit. This win marked his sixth consecutive triumph in the season and clinched his fourth consecutive World Drivers' Championship title, solidifying his dominance in Formula 1. Driving for Red Bull Racing, Vettel led the race from start to finish, showcasing his exceptional skill and the superior performance of his car. His victory in India was a key highlight in a season that saw him break numerous records and establish himself as one of the sport's greats.

2020
Lewis Hamilton's Record 7th Championship title

Lewis Hamilton clinched his record-tying seventh Formula 1 World Drivers' Championship title at the 2020 Turkish Grand Prix. With this achievement, he equaled the legendary Michael Schumacher's record, further cementing his status as one of the greatest drivers in the history of the sport. Despite challenging weather conditions and starting sixth on the grid, Hamilton delivered a masterful performance to win the race, showcasing his exceptional driving skills and strategic acumen. His 2020 season was marked by numerous victories and consistent excellence, highlighting his dominance and longevity in Formula 1.

2021
Rivalry between Hamilton and Verstappen

The rivalry between Lewis Hamilton and Max Verstappen has become one of the most intense and captivating in modern Formula 1. Starting in the 2021 season, the competition between the seasoned champion Hamilton and the young challenger Verstappen saw dramatic on-track battles and controversial incidents. Both drivers pushed their limits, resulting in several thrilling races and close finishes. The rivalry reached its peak during the 2021 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, where Verstappen secured his first World Championship title under highly contentious circumstances. This rivalry has not only heightened the excitement in the sport but also drawn global attention, showcasing the blend of talent, strategy, and fierce competition that defines Formula 1.

May 2024
Charles Leclerc's first home win

Charles Leclerc secured his first home win at the 2022 Monaco Grand Prix, a dream come true for the Monegasque driver. Racing for Scuderia Ferrari, Leclerc showcased his talent and determination on the iconic streets of Monte Carlo. His victory was especially significant, given the immense pressure of performing in front of his home crowd and the challenging nature of the circuit. This win marked a milestone in Leclerc's career, demonstrating his capability to triumph in one of the most prestigious and demanding races in the Formula 1 calendar.
